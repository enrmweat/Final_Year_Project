About

This page in introduction how QR Codes and Augmneted Reality are going to be introduced to Liverpool to make tourism easier. This project is going to aim to give more accessible information to Liverpool's best attractions by simply scanning a QR Code or using an Augmented Reality marker. More information on how this is going to work can be found below along with frequantly asked questions and a contact information.

QR Codes.Learn something new.

QR Codes like the one on the right can be found printed on plaques placed by Liverpools most iconic sights that can be found at the top of this website. Simply download a QR Code scanner such as Barcode Reader for android or download QR Reader for iOS. If these are downloaded quite simply scan the QR code found at the sight using the phone or tablets camera (Please note internet access will be required). The QR Code will show information on that sight giving easy and interesting information whilst the sight is viewed.

Augmented Reality. See stuff in a whole new way.

AR Markers just like QR Markers are placed at Liverpool's most popular tourist attractions. AR Markers just like the one shown on the left will display 3D Models of the attractions they are placed at when used with a mobile phone and its camera. AR markers may aslso be placed at places of historical interest to show augmented reality at its best by showing how Liverpool may have looked with an overhead railway or a tram line running through it.
